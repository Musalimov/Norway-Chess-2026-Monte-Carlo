§ B

Model & parameters

how the million tournaments were generated
classical  P(draw) = DBASE · exp(−|Δrating| · DDEC) · stylei · stylej
armageddon  played on blitz Elo · White carries ARM_H · Black has draw odds
form  after each classical game, effective Elo += K · (score − expected)

Parameters were fit on 180 games from the 2022–2026 events using RPS with MAP regularisation and leave-one-tournament-out cross-validation, then held fixed for the 2026 forecast.
